Jenny Gal
Country: Italy | Born: 2 November 1969 (53 years)
Jenny Gal is 1996 Olympic bronze medallist and 1995 World Championships finallist. Works as coach in Switzerland with her husband Giorgio Vismara. Sister of quadruple European Champion Jessica Gal. She has the responsibility of the U15 talents in Switzerland. Jenny was European champion in Birmingham in 1995 and claimed five European medals. She captured twelve World Cup gold medals including the Tournoi de Paris in 1989 for the Netherlands and 1999 for Italy. She lives with Giorgia Visamara as coaches in Switzerland.
