Patrick Klas
Country: Netherlands | Born: 22 November 1975 (47 years)
Olympic judoka Patrick Klas competed at the Olympic Games in 1996 in Atlanta U78kg. Klas won the Swiss International Basel U78kg in 1995. He won 10 Dutch senior medals including two titles in 1995 and 1999. He won national titles as Cadet and Junior. He was in the final of the Dutch Open twice.
