Jessica Gal
Country: Netherlands | Born: 6 July 1971 (52 years)
Dutch judoka Jessica Gal is 1999 World bronze medallist and sister of Jenny Gal. She participated at four Olympic Games between 1988-2000. She is a 4 time European Champion. Jessica claimed 13 national titles. She always trained at Kenamju but started in Amsterdam at Buenting. She became a Physiotherapist and later a sports doctor and opened her practise in 2017 in Haarlem. Before that she was very involved in various team sports as doctor. Gal has an American mother and Hungarian father.
