Robby van Laarhoven
Country: Netherlands | Born: 17 May 1982 (41 years)
Robby van Laarhoven was Dutch champion in 2011. He was 8 national medals in 3 different weight categories. He won national titles in all age categories. He won European Junior silver in 2001 and booked a valuable international victory in Hamburgs European Cup. Strong uchi-mata.
