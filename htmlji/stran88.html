Vincent van Kalken
Country: Netherlands | Born: 19 March 1978 (45 years)
Dutch judoka Vincent van Kalken won the European team title with the Netherlands in 1997. He won the European Cup with Kenamju in 1998. The lightweight became a policeman after his career. He won four Dutch bronze medals U60kg. Brother of Patrick van Kalken.
