Koen van Nol
Country: Netherlands | Born: 20 January 1974 (49 years)
Amsterdam born Koen van Nol was double Dutch senior champion, president of Kenamju who participated at World and European Championships. He won silver at the Kano Cup in 1999, European Team Champion Villach 1998. Favourite Throw: Harai Goshi. Started at Landsmeer, via Nieuwendam to Kenamju in 1992.
