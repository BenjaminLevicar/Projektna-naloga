Mark Huizinga
Country: Netherlands | Born: 10 September 1973 (50 years)
Mark Huizinga is 2000 Olympic Champion and triple medallist. He participated at Olympic Games from 1996-2008. 5 times European Champion. Hobbies: fishing and survival. He won 8 Military World titles and the Veteran World title in 2015. He was coached by Chris de Korte. President of the Survival Federation. Huizinga was inducted into the IJF Hall of Fame in 2018. He is ambassador of the IJF Academy. He was Holland’s Chef de Mission for the Youth Olympic Games in Buenos Aires.
