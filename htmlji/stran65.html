Judo career
Karin Kienhuis
Country: Netherlands | Born: 24 February 1971 (52 years)
Karin Kienhuis won 4 European medals and was a fixed member of the Dutch team taking 6 national titles. She fought the Paris final in 1996 and European Championships final in 1998. Lives and works in Groningen.
