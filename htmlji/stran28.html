Guillaume Elmont
Country: Netherlands | Born: 10 August 1981 (42 years)
Guillaume Elmont is 2005 World Champion and triple Olympic participant. Brother of Dex Elmont. Both from a judo family from Suriname. He won European Games bronze in 2015 in Baku, and 5 European medals in total. 2000 European junior Champion and won 11 national titles. He is physical coach at Ajax.
