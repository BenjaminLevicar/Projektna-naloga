Judo career
Bryan van Dijk
Country: Netherlands | Born: 8 February 1981 (42 years)
Dutch judoka Bryan van Dijk won a European bronze medal in 2006 in Tampere U73kg. He won European junior bronze in 2000. Trained by Theo Meijer and stepped over to Kenamju. Suffered a shoulder injury. He won Super World Cup in Hamburg and Dutch Open in 2006 and Grand Prix in Tunis in 2009. Now enterpreneur in gadgets.
