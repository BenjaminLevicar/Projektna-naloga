Edith Bosch
Country: Netherlands | Born: 31 May 1980 (43 years)
Edith Bosch is 2005 World Champion and triple Olympic medallist 2004-2012. Bosch retired in April 2013 with a unique European team title. She is four times European champion. She won 15 World Cups and 45 World Cup medals. Nickname: Bambi. Bosch was double World Junior champion. She won 15 World Cups including the Tournoi de Paris. She was coached by Chris de Korte.
